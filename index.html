Superior Finishing Process

Wood specially sourced from Brazil is shaped into a seat and sealed in a dipping tank using a high quality penetrating sealer.
The seat is then coated with multiple layers of paint and baked in an oven to ensure a hard, hygienic, long lasting, scratch resistant finish.

Original Equipment Manufacturer

We can take over at any stage in the design process to ensure that you get the best solution for your seat requirements. Creating either a contemporary or classic look, our seats will add value and complement the design of your pans.

Stainless Steel Hinges

Our hinge is manufactured using high grade 304 stainless steel. It is polished for several hours to achieve a smooth, high gloss finish and will never rust, peel nor break. The hinge is also fitted with a top fastening mechanism to ensure fast, hassle free fitment to the pan.
